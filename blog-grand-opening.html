Grand Opening of Overbound Game Studio
Post Date: 2015-11-18

This is the first post on my brand new studio website. Its an exciting time for me! Sonic Time Twisted is nearing completion and my next game No More Robots going into production shortly. I have decided that now is a good time to officially launch this studio website.

So welcome to Overbound Game Studio, I'll be updating the site with my new Pixel Art Tutorial videos and Developer Logs as well as the latest news on my game projects. Additionally I am interested in expanding the content I offer, such as a new video series or perhaps a smaller scale game. If you have any suggestions please let me know. Stay tuned for more updates!
